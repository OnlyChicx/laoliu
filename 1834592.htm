Shipping Terms

We offer combined shipping. Combined shipping saves your shipping cost greatly. You are allowed up to 7 days for your multiple purchases at Rikoo store.

How to calculate the combined shipping?
1) For Air (Standard): please deduct $6 for each additional item;
2) For Express (Expedited): please deduct $14 for each additional item.

How to pay the combined shipping? You have 3 options below:
1) Input the calculated combined shipping amount yourself when you check out;
2) Request an invoice from us. We will send you an invoice with combined shipping and you pay the invoice;
3) Just pay all the original shipping charges in the listings. And we will send you refund on shipping (according to the combined shipping rates) once we get your payment.

We ship the items the day or the next business day (mostly within 24 hours) of receipt of the payment.
